Lasagna Recipe

Comforting Lasagna recipe

A comforting lasagna recipe that always impress! layered, cheesy and packed with fich flavor. This lasagna turn any dinner into a cosy feast!

INGREDIENTS

Classic lasagna recipe ready in 1 Hour 15 Minutes

  • 12 Lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated permesan
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

STEPS

  1. Preheat oven to 190 degree celcius. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions, then drain.
  2. In a skillet, cook ground beef with onion until browned. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds.
  3. Stir in marinara sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan.
  5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish. Add noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce and mozzarella.
  6. Reapeat layers until ingredients are used.
  7. Sprinkle remaining mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  8. cover with foil and bake 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake 15 more minutes until bubbly and golden.
