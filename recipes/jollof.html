Jollof Rice Recipe

Best and Simple Jollof Rice Recipe.

If you want to make jollof rice, you should make it like this.

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 cups Long grain rice
  • 6 Fresh Tomatoes
  • 3 Red bell peppers
  • 1-2 scotch bonnet
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tbsp Tomato paste
  • 3-4 tbsp Vegetable oil
  • 2 Seasoning cube
  • 1 tsp Curry
  • 1 tsp Thyme
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 Bay leaf (optional)
  • 2-3 cups Stock (Chicken or beef)/water
  • 1 tbsp Butter (optional)

STEPS

  1. Blend tomatoes, red bell peppers, scotch bonnet and onion until smooth
  2. Heat oil in a pot. Fry tomato paste for 3-5mins. Add blended mix and cook for 15-20mins. Cook until thick and oil starts to separate.
  3. Add curry, thyme, seasoning cubes, salt and bay leaf. Stir well and cook for 5 mins. Taste and adjust.
  4. Add washed rice and stir to coat in the sauce. Pour in stock (just enough to cover the rice.) Stir well
  5. Cover the pot tightly with foil and lid. Cook on low heat for 20-30 mins. Stir occasionally. Add a little water if needed.
  6. Add butter (optional). Fluff rice gently. Allow the bottom to toast slighlty for that smoky, party jollof flavor!
  7. Serve with meat of choice, plantain, salad of choice or pepper sauce
