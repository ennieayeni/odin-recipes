Honey Garlic Butter Lamb Cutlets

Savour these honey garlic butter lamb cutlets.

Takes just 20 minutes to prep and cook. This easy and impressive dish is perfect for busy weeknights or special occasions.

INGREDIENTS

Meat

  • 12 Lamb cutlets
    •

    Produce

    • 3 Garlic cloves, whole
    • 6 Garlic cloves
    • 1 tbsp Garlic powder
    • 1 tbsp Onion powder
    • 1 1/2 tsp Oregano, dried
    • 1 Parsley, fresh
    • 3 Sprigs Rosemary, fresh

    Condiments

    • 1 tbsp Honey
    • 1 tbsp Lemon juice
    • 1 tsp Soy sauce
    • 1 1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce

    Baking & Spices

    • 1 Black pepper
    • 1 tbsp Brown sugar
    • 1 tbsp Paprika, sweet
    • 1 Salt

    Fat

    • 7 1/2 tsp Olive oil
    • 1/4 cup butter

    STEPS

    1. Toss the lamb cutlets with the spices and olive oil until they are well coated, then let them marinate for at least 15 minutes (or overnight preferably).
    2. Heat olive oil in a large pan, then cook the lam cutlets for 1-2 minutes on the first side, then flip and cook for 30 seconds on the other side. Add the butter, garlic and rosemary and base the meat with the melted butter for 1 min.
    3. Remove the meat, garlic and rosemary from the pan and combine the glaze ingredients in the pan. Stir and cook until it's thickened, about 3 minutes.
    4. Coat the lamb cutlets in the sauce for about 1 minute on each side to heat them through.
    5. Let the lamb cutlets rest for 5 minutes, theb serve with your favourite sides and enjoy straight away.
